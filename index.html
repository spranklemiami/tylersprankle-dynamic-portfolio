About Me

After learning the hard way, how to pick and choose my battles, I now strive for a more light-hearted lifestyle. I joke often, I laugh alot and I love helping others. I've found that assisting others with their endeavors helps relieve me from my own, temporarily. It's a nice break. A place where I can learn stresslessly.

If anyone out there needs an extra set of hands, thats what I'm here for. A wise man once told me, "If you can't be used, you are useless."
